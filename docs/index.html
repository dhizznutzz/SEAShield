SeaShield — AI Payment Guard

A safety layer that inspects a checkout or invoice before you authorise a payment, and warns you if it looks like fraud.

Pick a demo below and click the payment button. On a suspicious page, SeaShield pauses the payment and shows a risk score with the reasons why. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is installed and no data leaves this page.

How it works

  1. When you click a Pay / Authorise button, SeaShield intercepts it before the payment happens.
  2. It reads the page — the site domain, payee, amount, bank details and any payment links.
  3. A heuristics + reputation engine scores the risk from 0–100: 0–30 safe, 31–70 caution, 71–100 danger.
  4. Low risk proceeds silently. Anything higher shows a warning; high-risk payments require you to type PROCEED.

About this demo: the real SeaShield is a Chrome extension backed by a cloud AI service. This page is a self-contained port of its heuristics engine so anyone can try the flow with no setup. To run the full extension (with the AI layer) locally, clone the repo and follow seashield-payguard/README.md.

SeaShield is an assistant, not a guarantee — you always make the final call.
